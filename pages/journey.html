The Journey

My cricket journey started when I first picked up a bat. Since then, I’ve been learning, practicing, and competing at every opportunity.

I’ve played in school teams, local tournaments, and indoor sessions. Each experience has taught me new skills, teamwork, and discipline.

Along the way, I’ve faced challenges and celebrated victories. Every match, whether I batted, kept wickets, or fielded, has contributed to my growth as a player.

I continue to train, refine my skills, and aim for higher goals. My journey is ongoing, and I love every step of it!
